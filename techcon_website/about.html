History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and educators passionate about open-source and innovation. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for sharing ideas, launching products, and shaping the future of technology.

Mission

TechCon’s mission is to empower creators, educators, and technologists to build inclusive, impactful solutions. We believe in the power of collaboration, continuous learning, and ethical innovation.
